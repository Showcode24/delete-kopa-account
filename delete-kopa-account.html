Delete Your Kopa360 Account

To request the deletion of your account and associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your Kopa360 account from the app.
  2. Navigate to "Account Settings" and select "Delete Account."
  3. Confirm your request, and we will process it within 7 business days.

Alternatively, you can contact us at: info@kopa360.com for assistance.
